The App Store version

File13 is also distributed as a notarized build on the Mac App Store. That build is a paid convenience — a one-time File13 Pro in-app purchase unlocks unlimited mailboxes and the bundled file13 command-line tool. Buying it supports development; it is not required to use the software. The complete source is available under the MIT License above, and you are free to build it yourself at no cost.

Operating responsibly

File13 performs operations that can permanently alter your mailboxes — deleting, archiving, moving messages, and changing flags. Confirmation prompts, a buffered undo window, dry-run modes, and VIP protections are aids, not guarantees. Likewise, any AI-assisted suggestion is advisory and may be wrong. You remain responsible for every action you take, and for keeping backups of anything you cannot afford to lose. As stated above, the software is provided without warranty of any kind.
